Fundoo Carousel Directive

The Fundoo Carousel is heavily inspired and liberally borrows from Cubiq's SwipeView, but has been heavily modified to integrate with AngularJS.

This carousel is awesome because

  • Light on DOM- Uses rotating three divs by default
  • Lazy loading - Only loads the next and previous page, and loads the rest as needed
  • Directive - Easily reusable directive, that gives you all the hooks you need
